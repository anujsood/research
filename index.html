I am the Research Development Manager for the Department of Mathematics, Imperial College London. I have been at Imperial in various managerial roles at the Department of Mathematics, Medicine and Computing since 2014 apart from running two tech projects. Previously, I have worked in academia for Brunel University and Nanoforce (a Queen Mary spin-out), and large companies such as Boots and Steria. I am a qualified chartered management accountant (2013) and PRINCE2 practitioner (2012), and hold degrees in Management (MBA, 2006) and Engineering (2001).
